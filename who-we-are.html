Who we are...

From Comunity driven ROM to Comunity driven website...

Welcome to the universe of OwnDroid! Some of you might still know me/us from our beloved custom ROM OwnROM, while this project was fun to do, I simply don't have the time anymore to build and maintain this ROM. I know many of you might find this a disapointmend but I still want to do something for the comunity in a good way and bringing Android to a whole new level. 

OwnROM was a comunity build ROM where users could decide with us what features to use in the ROM itself and what not, with user friendly Developers who even accept ETA questions. This will be the main idea to continue in this website. A comunity driven website with only friendly advices from experienced users. I will keep a strict eye out on this and will reward everybody who lives by this rule! So don't be affraid to ask anything you like to know!


The main goal for us is to create not only a noob friendly website, but also your one stop website to find everything you need to know ar want to have, such as apps, mods, custom ROMs, reviews about them and wallpapers.

But it doesn't stop there...

Did you made your own ROM, mod, app, wall or review? Please feel free get in touch with me troug the CONTACT Page... I'm giving everybody who thinks they have something to contribute a stage to get it out there! So do you have something to share? Bring it on!
